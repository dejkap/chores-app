Project Overview & Philosophy

Chores-App is a privacy-centric task management ecosystem designed to transform mundane household maintenance into a competitive, gamified experience.

The core philosophy addresses two modern challenges:

  • Data Sovereignty: Unlike mainstream productivity apps, Chores-App is built for users who want to manage their private household routines without sharing behavioral data with third-party corporations.
  • Domestic Friction: By applying RPG-style mechanics to household chores, we turn “who’s turn it is to wash the dishes” into a strategic competition, embodying the “Chores as a Sport” concept.

Current MVP Status (Phase 0)

The application currently serves as a functional proof of concept with a strong technical foundation emphasizing privacy and data integrity:

  • Full CRUD lifecycle: create, edit, delete, and complete tasks.
  • Three-tier priority system (Low, Normal, High).
  • Real-time persistence via Supabase backend.
  • Bi-directional calendar synchronization.
  • QA Lab environment for tracking happy, negative, and edge-case paths.

Extended Roadmap (The Vision)

Built on a privacy-first mission and the idea of “Chores as a Sport,” the roadmap unfolds across multiple phases:

Phase 1: Multi-user Foundation & Household Structure
User identity with profiles and avatars; household hierarchy by rooms; shared state dashboard for real-time visibility.

Phase 2: Logic, Routine & Verification
Advanced recurrence engine (daily to yearly); photo proof layer for completion verification; comprehensive task history logs.

Phase 3: The Gamification Economy
XP and rewards, marketplace for postponing or delegating tasks; dual rule modes—“Free Hand” vs. “Hard Rules”—where points govern flexibility.

Phase 4: Analytics & Competitive Chores
Leaderboards, hot streak bonuses, Focus Mode (timer, anti‑distraction lock, personal bests) turning chores into measurable athletic events.

Phase 5: Engagement & Retention
Achievement milestones, educational fun facts, and AI-driven personalized insights to maintain long-term engagement.

Technical Stack (Current)

  • Frontend: React with Tailwind CSS
  • Backend/Auth: Supabase
  • Hosting/Environment: Vercel
